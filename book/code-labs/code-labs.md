## Available Resources

All resources in this portal are tagged and filterable by **six** distinct and high-level water subject topics:
- **Hydroinformatics \& Data Science**: Application of computer science, data processing, and information technology to water-related data. <span style="background-color: #455A64; color: white; padding: 2px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.8em; font-weight: bold; margin: 0 4px;">Hydroinformatics</span>
- **Hydrologic Modeling**: Theoretical frameworks and computational tools used to simulate and forecast water system behaviors. <span style="background-color: #2E7D32; color: white; padding: 2px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.8em; font-weight: bold; margin: 0 4px;">Modeling</span>
- **Hydrometeorology & Climatology**: Transfer of water and energy between the land surface and the lower atmosphere. <span style="background-color: #008080; color: white; padding: 2px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.8em; font-weight: bold; margin: 0 4px;">Hydrometeorology</span>
- **Surface Water Hydrology**: Distribution, movement, and quality of water on the Earth's surface. <span style="background-color: #1976D2; color: white; padding: 2px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.8em; font-weight: bold; margin: 0 4px;">Surface Water</span>
- **Groundwater & Subsurface Hydrology**: Occurrence, flow, and quality of water beneath the Earth's surface. <span style="background-color: #8D6E63; color: white; padding: 2px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.8em; font-weight: bold; margin: 0 4px;">Groundwater</span>
- **Remote Sensing & Earth Observation**: Use of satellite, airborne, and drone-based sensor data to monitor and analyze water resources. <span style="background-color: #7B1FA2; color: white; padding: 2px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.8em; font-weight: bold; margin: 0 4px;">Remote Sensing</span>
